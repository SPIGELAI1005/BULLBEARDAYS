## How to Analyze Charts

### Method 1: Upload Chart Image

1. **Click** the upload area or press `Ctrl + U`
2. **Select** a chart screenshot from your device
3. **Supported formats:** PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP
4. **Max size:** 10MB

### Method 2: Chat Mode (with Context)

1. **Toggle** "Chat Mode" on
2. **Paste** an image directly (Ctrl + V)
3. **Add context:** Describe what you want to analyze
   - Example: "Is this a bullish breakout?"
   - Example: "Should I enter this trade on BTC 4H?"
4. **Click** "Analyze Chart" or press `Ctrl + Enter`

### Method 3: Multi-Chart Upload

1. **Click** the layers icon to enable multi-chart mode
2. **Upload** multiple charts (up to 5)
3. **Analyze** them together for comprehensive insights

### Method 4: Market Data Analysis

1. **Browse** the live market ticker
2. **Click** "Analyze" on any asset
3. Get instant AI signals based on current market conditions

## Understanding Signals

### Signal Types

#### üü¢ BUY Signal
- **Meaning:** Strong bullish momentum detected
- **Action:** Consider entering a long position
- **Confidence:** 70-100% = High conviction

#### üî¥ SELL Signal
- **Meaning:** Strong bearish momentum detected
- **Action:** Consider entering a short position or exiting longs
- **Confidence:** 70-100% = High conviction

#### üü° HOLD Signal
- **Meaning:** Consolidation or unclear direction
- **Action:** Wait for better setup
- **Confidence:** Usually 40-60%

### Analysis Components

#### 1. Probability Score
- **Range:** 0-100%
- **Interpretation:**
  - 80-100%: Very high confidence
  - 60-79%: High confidence
  - 40-59%: Moderate confidence
  - Below 40%: Low confidence (avoid)

#### 2. Take Profit (TP)
- Suggested profit target as percentage
- Example: "+5.2%" means exit at 5.2% gain
- **Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive** targets available

#### 3. Stop Loss (SL)
- Risk management level as percentage
- Example: "-2.1%" means exit at 2.1% loss
- **Always use stop losses!**

#### 4. Risk/Reward Ratio
- **Format:** "1:2.5"
- **Meaning:** For every $1 risked, potential $2.50 gain
- **Minimum recommended:** 1:2

#### 5. Chart Analysis
- Technical analysis of patterns, indicators, and price action
- Identifies support/resistance levels
- Detects chart patterns (triangles, flags, head & shoulders)

#### 6. Market Sentiment
- Overall market context
- Macro trend analysis
- Volume analysis

#### 7. Bullish vs Bearish Reasons
- **Bullish Points:** Why price might go up
- **Bearish Points:** Why price might go down
- Helps you understand both sides of the trade

## Performance Tracking

### Recording Outcomes

After a trade closes:

1. **Open** the analysis from History panel
2. **Click** "Edit" or "Record Outcome"
3. **Select:** WIN, LOSS, or PENDING
4. **Add notes** (optional)
5. **Save**

### Dashboard Metrics

- **Win Rate:** % of profitable trades
- **Total Trades:** Number of analyzed charts
- **Best Performer:** Asset with highest win rate
- **Profit Factor:** Total wins √∑ Total losses
- **Average R/R:** Average risk/reward ratio

### Charts & Analytics

- **Win/Loss over time:** Track your improvement
- **Performance by asset:** Which markets work best for you
- **Strategy comparison:** Compare different approaches
- **Monthly breakdown:** See trends month-over-month

## Watchlist & Alerts

### Creating a Watchlist

1. **Analyze** any asset
2. **Click** "Add to Watchlist"
3. **Add notes** about why you're watching it
4. **Access** watchlist from the sidebar

### Setting Price Alerts

1. **Open** Price Alerts panel
2. **Click** "Add Alert"
3. **Enter:**
   - Asset name (e.g., "BTC/USDT")
   - Target price
   - Alert type (Above/Below)
4. **Save**

You'll be notified when price hits your target!

### Managing Your Watchlist

- **Edit notes:** Click the pencil icon
- **Remove items:** Click the trash icon
- **Re-analyze:** Click "Analyze" for updated signals
- **Sort:** By date added or asset name
